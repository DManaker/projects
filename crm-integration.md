# HubSpot Integration: Centralizing Product & Customer Data for GTM Scale

## ✖️ Problem
As KARE scaled, critical customer and product data was fragmented across multiple systems. HubSpot, our primary CRM, lacked real‑time visibility into in‑product activity, limiting its usefulness for:
* Sales prioritization and pipeline management
* Marketing automation and campaign targeting
* Accurate reporting and operational decision‑making

This disconnect created a clear business constraint: sales and marketing teams were operating without reliable, product‑level signals, slowing GTM execution and reducing the effectiveness of downstream automation.

## 💡 Solution
After being promoted to Product Manager, I led a 4‑month initiative to bridge the gap between KARE’s product data and HubSpot by integrating HubSpot’s APIs directly into the platform.
I partnered closely with development, sales operations, and marketing to:
* Audit existing data structures and identify high‑value data points needed for sales and marketing workflows
* Define ownership, data contracts, and synchronization rules between systems
* Create user stories, workflow diagrams, and UAT plans to ensure data accuracy and reliability

## 💥 Results and Impact
* Enabled increased prospect pipeline generation through improved visibility into customer behavior
* Supported weekly targeted marketing campaigns using reliable, product‑driven signals
& Improved data accuracy and trust across sales, marketing, and operations
* Created a standardized audit log that reduced manual investigation and reporting overhead
