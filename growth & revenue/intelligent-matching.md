# Increasing Marketplace Conversion Through Intelligent Matching

## ✖️ Problem
Marketplace revenue was suffering due to declining conversion across the clinician funnel. Data revealed two major friction points:
* Clinicians struggled to efficiently find relevant shifts after logging in
* Drop‑off increased significantly after clinicians were rejected for shifts, especially among first‑time users

Despite strong demand, our platform lacked personalization and intelligent ordering. Filters were minimal and static, behavioral signals were underutilized, and rejected clinicians received no guidance toward alternative opportunities resulting in abandonment and lost revenue.

This created a clear opportunity to improve experience while recovering marketplace liquidity and conversion.

## 💡 Solution
I owned end‑to‑end discovery through delivery of two complementary initiatives:
* Intelligent shift matching based on clinician preferences and behavioral signals
* Re‑engagement workflows that redirected rejected clinicians to relevant opportunities instead of dead ends

Together, these projects transformed shift discovery from a passive browse experience into an active, guided system. My responsibilities included:
* Conducting competitive research to identify effective marketplace matching patterns
* Running clinician surveys and interviews to identify high‑signal browsing and application criteria
* Designing UI/UX flows that captured preference data without adding friction
* Translating insights into technical requirements and delivery tickets
* Scoping MVPs and leading phased post‑launch iteration
* Designing internal support and account‑management visibility into clinician preferences

## 💥 Impacts & Results
* Increased application distribution across shifts, reducing over‑concentration on a small subset
* Improved conversion from posted → applied → worked shifts, directly driving revenue growth
* Reduced time‑to‑value from login to application through guided discovery
* Generated higher‑quality behavioral data to inform future personalization and marketplace optimization
* Increased overall shift fill rates while improving clinician experience and retention
